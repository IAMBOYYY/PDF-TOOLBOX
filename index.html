Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Toolbox safe to use?

Yes. Since all processing is done locally in your browser (client-side), your files are never transmitted over the internet to a server. This is the safest way to handle PDFs online.

How much does it cost?

PDF Toolbox is 100% free with no hidden subscriptions, no file size limits, and no "pro" versions.

Can I merge encrypted PDFs?

Standard password-protected PDFs must be unlocked before merging. Our tool currently supports standard PDFs for merging and splitting.
